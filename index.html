Operation:True Love

Operation True Love is a popular webtoon series that blends romance, comedy, and drama into an engaging story. The plot revolves around a high school girl, who is determined to win the heart of her crush, only to find herself tangled in a series of hilarious and unexpected situations. With its charming characters and witty humor, the series explores themes of love, friendship, and self-discovery. Each episode presents a fresh twist, keeping readers hooked with its mix of heartfelt moments and lighthearted fun. Operation True Love offers an entertaining experience for fans of romantic comedies, leaving them eagerly awaiting the next chapter.

Characters

Shim Su-ae
Shim Su-ae is the protagonist who finds herself stuck in a long-term, one-sided relationship with her indifferent boyfriend, Minu Kang. Despite her feelings for him, Su-ae struggles with self-doubt, wishing she could emulate her confident stepsister, Ra-im Yun. Her life takes an unexpected turn when she discovers Jellypop, a sentient flip phone, which provides guidance on her complicated love life. As Su-ae's relationship with Minu continues to unravel, she finds herself growing closer to Minu's friend, Eunhyeok Go, who challenges her perception of love.

Go Eunhyuk
Go Eunhyeok is one of Su-ae's friends and a significant love interest in the series. Initially, he views Su-ae with disdain for remaining in her toxic relationship with Minu. However, as they spend more time together, Eunhyeok begins to appreciate Su-ae's sincerity and genuine nature. His feelings for her deepen, and he eventually confesses his love, though Su-ae struggles to reciprocate due to the complexities of her situation.

Baek Dohwa
Baek Dohwa is another pivotal character in Operation: True Love, serving as another potential love interest for Su-ae. Handsome and popular, Dohwa is rumored to be an idol trainee and the son of a wealthy family, which only adds to his mystique. However, as Su-ae gets to know him better, she discovers his more humble lifestyle. Dohwa develops feelings for Su-ae and, after some time, begins to subtly pursue her, competing with Eunhyeok for her affections.
